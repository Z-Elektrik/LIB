
## *Skratky v nazvoch*
P = Pitch of components with more than two leads

W = component lead diameter (or maximum lead width)

L = Body length for **_horizontal _**installation

D = diameter of the round component body

T = thickness of rectangular components body

H = the height of the vertically mounted component

Q = Number of pins for components with more than two Leads

R = Number of lines in the connector
